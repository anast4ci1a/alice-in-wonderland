Characters from Alice in Wonderland

Alice

  • The protagonist, a curious and adventurous young girl who falls down a rabbit hole into Wonderland.
  • She navigates the strange and fantastical world with a blend of logic and imagination.

The White Rabbit

  • A nervous, hurried rabbit who serves as Alice's guide into Wonderland.
  • Famous for his catchphrase, "Oh dear! Oh dear! I shall be too late!"

The Cheshire Cat

  • A grinning cat who can appear and disappear at will.
  • Known for his cryptic and philosophical advice to Alice.

The Queen of Hearts

  • The tyrannical ruler of Wonderland who is quick to decree death sentences with her famous line, "Off with their heads!"
  • Despite her threats, her authority is often undermined by her own inconsistencies and the actions of others.

The Mad Hatter

  • A madcap hat maker who hosts a never-ending tea party.
  • He is eccentric, and his conversations are marked by nonsensical riddles and paradoxes.
